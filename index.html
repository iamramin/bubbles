Bubbles!

A free and simple bubble popping app

A free and simple bubble popping app for all ages.

  • Free with no ads or in-app purchases
  • Beautiful burst animation with 'pop' sound
  • Background changes to a night sky after 7pm and before 7am
  • Blow into the device microphone for even more bubbles
Bubbles is an open-source project, so you can view and contribute to the source code over on GitHub
